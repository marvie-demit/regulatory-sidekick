1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures critical services and data can be recovered within defined objectives after disruption.

Scope: continuity and disaster recovery for critical processes and the cloud/IT environment. It satisfies ISO/IEC 27001 A.5.29-30 and SOC 2 availability criteria.
