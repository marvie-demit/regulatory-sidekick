1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures a device is conformity-assessed, documented and registered before being placed on the EU market, and the Technical Documentation is compiled and maintained.

Scope: MDR market access (EU MDR Art 10, 19, 20, 52; Annex II/III).

2 Roles & Responsibilities
