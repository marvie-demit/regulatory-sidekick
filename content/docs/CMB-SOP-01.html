1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure manages the device constituent of a drug-device combination and the device-medicinal interface.

Scope: drug-device combinations (e.g. pre-filled syringes, drug-eluting devices, inhalers, injectors). DORMANT until applicable. It satisfies MDR Art 117 (notified-body opinion), ISO 11608 (needle-based injection systems) and the relevant medicinal-product requirements.
