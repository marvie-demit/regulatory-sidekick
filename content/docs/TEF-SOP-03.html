1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures an IVD device is classified, conformity-assessed, documented and registered before being placed on the EU market.

Scope: IVDR market access (EU IVDR Art 10, 17, 18, 48; Annex II/III). Mirrors TEF-SOP-02 with IVDR specifics.
