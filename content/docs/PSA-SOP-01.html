What & whyHow you identify, segregate and safely handle product that is or may be contaminated, so it cannot cause harm or be used by mistake.
When to useWhen contamination is detected or suspected in production, returns or the field. Dormant for software/IVD-only product.
WhoProduction/QC owns identification & segregation; the QMR authorises the disposition.
How to use itIdentify and segregate the contaminated product, assess the hazard, decontaminate or dispose by a defined method, record the disposition, and feed issues to risk and CAPA.
What auditors checkContaminated product clearly segregated and identified; disposition justified and recorded; staff/handling protected.
Pitfalls & relatedPitfalls: contaminated returns re-entering usable stock. Related: NCP-SOP-01, RSK-SOP-01, PRO-SOP-01.

1 Purpose & Scope

Guidance (delete before release): 2-3 sentences: what this ensures and where it applies.

This procedure defines how [Organisation] identifies, segregates and safely handles product that is or may be contaminated, to protect patients, users and personnel and to prevent contaminated product re-entering usable stock. It applies whenever contamination is detected or suspected in production, returns or service.
