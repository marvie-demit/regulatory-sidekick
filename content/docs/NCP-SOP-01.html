1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures product or output that does not conform to requirements is identified, segregated, evaluated and dispositioned to prevent its unintended use or delivery. For software, 'nonconforming product' includes nonconforming builds, releases and outputs.

Scope: all nonconforming product/output across the lifecycle. It satisfies ISO 13485 8.3 (mandatory documented procedure).
