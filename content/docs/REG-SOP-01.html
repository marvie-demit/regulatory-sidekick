1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures applicable regulations, standards and guidance are monitored, their impact assessed, and compliance and product registrations maintained.

Scope: all markets in which product is placed. It supports ISO 13485 4.1/4.2 and MDR/IVDR market obligations.
