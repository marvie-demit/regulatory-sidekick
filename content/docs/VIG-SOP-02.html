1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures serious incidents and field safety corrective actions are evaluated and reported to the competent authorities within the required timelines.

Scope: all marketed devices in the EU. It satisfies EU MDR Art 87-92 (IVDR Art 82-87 for IVD).

2 Roles & Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
RA ManagerOwns vigilance; assesses reportability and submits reports.
QMREnsures incidents from complaints/PMS reach vigilance.
