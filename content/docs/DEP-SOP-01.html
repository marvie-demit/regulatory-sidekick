1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure controls how released software is deployed, monitored, maintained and rolled back in production (incl. cloud), as the SaMD equivalent of production/service provision.

Scope: release-to-production, deployment, monitoring, maintenance and patching of SaMD. It satisfies ISO 13485 7.5 and EU MDR Annex I §17, and integrates security (SEC-SOP-01) and AI (AIM-SOP-01).
