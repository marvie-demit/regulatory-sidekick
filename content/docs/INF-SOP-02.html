1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures that monitoring and measuring equipment - and any device function used to make measurements - provides valid results, through calibration/verification traceable to measurement standards.

Scope: monitoring & measuring equipment used in production/QC, and devices with a measuring function. DORMANT for products with no measuring function and no measuring equipment. It satisfies ISO 13485 7.6 and MDR GSPR 15.2.
