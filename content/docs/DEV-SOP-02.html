1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure adapts design & development for IVD assays and instruments, integrating analytical and clinical performance establishment.

Scope: IVD product development. It satisfies ISO 13485 7.3 and IVDR Annex II; it extends DEV-SOP-03 with IVD specifics.
