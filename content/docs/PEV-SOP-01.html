1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures the performance of an IVD is demonstrated through scientific validity, analytical performance and clinical performance, and maintained post-market.

Scope: performance evaluation for IVDR devices (incl. IVD software). It satisfies EU IVDR Annex XIII and follows ISO 20916 and GHTF SG5.

3.3 Analytical performance

InputAssay/algorithm; samples
Activity• Establish analytical performance (e.g. sensitivity, specificity, precision, robustness, stability).
• For software IVD, establish the analytical performance of the algorithm.
• Run studies per ISO 20916 with defined acceptance criteria.
