1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure extends the integrated risk management process (RSK-SOP-01) with IVD-specific considerations, in particular risks arising from incorrect results and their clinical consequences.

Scope: IVD devices. It satisfies ISO 14971 and ISO/TR 22367, applied per RSK-SOP-01.
