1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures the work environment needed for product conformity is defined, controlled and monitored.

Scope: work-environment requirements (ISO 13485 6.4). For software-only products, physical environment and contamination controls are minimal and documented as a justified non-application where appropriate.
