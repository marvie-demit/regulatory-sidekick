1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures post-market data are proactively and systematically collected and reviewed to confirm ongoing safety and performance, to update the risk and clinical files, and to trigger action where needed.

Scope: all marketed devices. It satisfies ISO 13485 8.2.1 and EU MDR Art 83-86 (IVDR Art 78-81 handled in PMS-SOP-02).
