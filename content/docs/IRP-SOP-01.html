1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures security, privacy and operational incidents are detected, contained, resolved and reported within required timelines.

Scope: all security/privacy/operational incidents. It satisfies ISO/IEC 27001 A.5.24-28, SOC 2 CC7 and GDPR Art 33/34; device-safety incidents are routed to vigilance (VIG-SOP-02).
