1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure manages the design, manufacture, statement and traceability of custom-made / patient-matched devices.

Scope: custom-made and patient-matched devices (incl. 3D-printed). DORMANT until applicable. It satisfies MDR Annex XIII (custom-made devices) and is produced within a validated design envelope.
