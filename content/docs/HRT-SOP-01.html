1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures personnel whose work affects product and IMS conformity are competent on the basis of education, training, skills and experience, including information-security awareness and AI competence.

Scope: all personnel and contractors performing IMS-relevant work. It satisfies ISO 13485 6.2, ISO 27001 7.2/7.3 and ISO 42001 7.2.
