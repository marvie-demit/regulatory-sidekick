1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures clinical safety and performance are proactively confirmed in the post-market phase and the findings update the clinical evaluation and risk file.

Scope: PMCF for MDR devices (EU MDR Annex XIV Part B; MDCG 2020-7/2020-8).

2 Roles & Responsibilities
