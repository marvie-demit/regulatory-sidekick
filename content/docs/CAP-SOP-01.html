1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures nonconformities and potential nonconformities from any source are recorded, investigated to root cause, corrected and prevented from recurring, with the effectiveness of actions verified.

Scope: all sources of nonconformity across the IMS - audits, complaints, PMS, security/operational incidents, risk reviews and supplier issues. It satisfies ISO 13485 8.5, ISO 27001 cl.10 and ISO 42001 cl.10.

3 Procedure

3.1 Capture & contain

InputNonconformity from audit, complaint, PMS, incident, risk or supplier
Activity• Record the nonconformity in the CAPA List with source, description and date.
• Apply immediate correction/containment to limit impact and stop further nonconforming output.
• Notify the QMR.
ResponsibleInitiator (R); QMR (A)
Method / CriteriaAll nonconformities captured; containment before root-cause work
Tools / TemplateCAP-LIS-01; CAP-FOR-01
Output / RecordOpened CAPA record; correction
ClauseISO 13485 8.3 / 8.5.2

3.2 Evaluate need

InputOpen CAPA record
Activity• Assess the significance and the risk of the issue.
• Decide, on a risk basis, whether a formal CAPA is required and set its priority.
• Where no further action is taken, record the justification.
ResponsibleQMR (R)
Method / CriteriaRisk-based decision
Tools / TemplateCAP-FOR-01
Output / RecordEvaluation & decision
ClauseISO 13485 8.5.1

3.3 Root-cause analysis

InputCAPA requiring action
Activity• Investigate using a structured method (e.g. 5-Whys, Ishikawa).
• Confirm the true root cause with objective evidence rather than treating symptoms.
• Determine whether the cause exists elsewhere (extent of the problem).
ResponsibleProcess Owner (R); QMR (Support)
Method / CriteriaEvidence-based; structured RCA
Tools / TemplateCAP-FOR-01
Output / RecordRoot-cause statement
ClauseISO 13485 8.5.2 b)

3.4 Plan actions

InputRoot cause
Activity• Define corrective actions that eliminate the root cause.
• For potential nonconformities, define preventive actions.
• Assign owners and due dates; identify documents and risk files needing update.
• Ensure the action is proportionate to the risk.
ResponsibleProcess Owner (R); QMR (A)
Method / CriteriaActions proportionate to risk
Tools / TemplateCAP-FOR-01
Output / RecordAction plan
ClauseISO 13485 8.5.2 / 8.5.3

3.5 Implement

InputAction plan
Activity• Implement the planned actions.
• Update affected documents via Change Management (CHG-SOP-01) and risk files (RSK-SOP-01).
• Deliver any required training.
ResponsibleProcess Owner (R)
Method / CriteriaPer approved plan
Tools / TemplateCHG-SOP-01; RSK-SOP-01
Output / RecordImplemented actions
ClauseISO 13485 8.5

3.6 Verify effectiveness & close

InputImplemented actions
Activity• Verify the actions were effective against defined success criteria.
• Confirm no adverse effects were introduced.
• Close the CAPA; record lessons learned and feed trends to management review.
ResponsibleQMR (R)
Method / CriteriaEffectiveness checked against success criteria
Tools / TemplateCAP-LIS-01
Output / RecordEffectiveness check; closed CAPA
ClauseISO 13485 8.5.2 e)
