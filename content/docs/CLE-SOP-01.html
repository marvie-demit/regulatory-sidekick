1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures clinical data are planned, gathered, appraised and analysed to demonstrate conformity with the relevant general safety and performance requirements and an acceptable benefit-risk profile.

Scope: clinical evaluation across the lifecycle for MDR devices. It satisfies EU MDR Art 61 & Annex XIV and follows MEDDEV 2.7/1 rev4 and MDCG 2020-1.

2 Roles & Responsibilities

3.1 Clinical evaluation plan

InputIntended use; risk file; state of the art
Activity• Define the scope and the clinical claims to be substantiated.
• Define the evaluation strategy, the equivalence approach (if used) and the acceptance criteria for benefit-risk.
• Identify the data sources to be searched.
