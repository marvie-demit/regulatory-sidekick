1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures active (powered) medical devices meet basic safety and essential performance - electrical, mechanical, thermal and EMC - and are type-tested to the IEC 60601 series.

Scope: electrically powered medical devices (and IEC 61010 lab equipment). DORMANT until the product includes active hardware. It satisfies IEC 60601-1, IEC 60601-1-2 (EMC) and applicable particular standards (60601-2-x).
