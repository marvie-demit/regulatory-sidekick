1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures customer support and servicing are delivered, recorded, and fed back into the QMS as feedback and complaints.

Scope: all support and servicing activities. It satisfies ISO 13485 7.5.4 and feeds 8.2.1/8.2.2.

2 Roles & Responsibilities

Support LeadOwns support/servicing delivery and records.
QMREnsures feedback reaches PMS and complaint handling.
