1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure applies post-market surveillance to IVD devices, mirroring PMS-SOP-01 with IVDR-specific reporting and post-market performance follow-up.

Scope: marketed IVD devices. It satisfies EU IVDR Art 78-81 and feeds the performance evaluation (PEV-SOP-01).
