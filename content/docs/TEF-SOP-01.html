What & whyHow you place a device on the US market - choosing the FDA route (510(k)/De Novo/PMA) and registering & listing it.
When to useWhen you market a device in the USA. Dormant until you target the US market.
WhoThe RA Manager owns the FDA pathway and submissions.
How to use itDetermine the FDA pathway, compile the submission (510(k)/De Novo/PMA), register the establishment and list the device, and maintain US-specific labelling (incl. FD&C 524B cybersecurity).
What auditors checkCorrect pathway determination; cleared/approved status before marketing; establishment registration & device listing current.
Pitfalls & relatedPitfalls: marketing before clearance; missing FDA cybersecurity (524B). Related: VIG-SOP-01, 13_US-FDA-Market, 21 CFR 820/QMSR.

1 Purpose & Scope

Guidance (delete before release): 2-3 sentences: what this ensures and where it applies.

This procedure defines how [Organisation] places a device on the U.S. market: determining the FDA regulatory pathway, compiling and submitting the marketing application, and confirming cleared or approved status before distribution. It applies to any device marketed in the USA.
