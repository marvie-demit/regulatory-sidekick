1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures device labeling and Unique Device Identification meet regulatory requirements and support traceability.

Scope: labeling and UDI for all marketed devices. It satisfies MDR Annex I Ch III and Art 27 (IVDR Annex I / Art 24).

2 Roles & Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
RA ManagerOwns labeling content and UDI assignment/registration.
DevelopmentImplements labeling in the product.
QMRControls labeling changes.

• Address language requirements per target markets.
