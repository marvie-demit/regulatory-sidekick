1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures personal data are processed lawfully, fairly and securely, and that data-subject rights are upheld.

Scope: all processing of personal (incl. health) data. It satisfies GDPR and, where applicable, HIPAA, and follows EDPB guidance; it uses ISO/IEC 27018/27701 and ISO 27799 for controls. It runs alongside ISM-SOP-01.
