1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures customer requirements are determined and reviewed before commitment, and that the organisation can meet them.

Scope: customer-related processes - enquiries, tenders, quotes and contracts. It satisfies ISO 13485 7.2.

2 Roles & Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
Sales LeadOwns customer communication, tenders and contract review.
QMR / DevelopmentConfirm feasibility and regulatory constraints.
