1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures devices supplied sterile are sterilized by a validated process and packaged to establish and maintain a sterile barrier to the point of use.

Scope: devices supplied sterile or reprocessed. DORMANT until the product is sterile. It satisfies ISO 11135 (EO), ISO 11137 (radiation), ISO 17665 (moist heat) and ISO 11607 (packaging).
