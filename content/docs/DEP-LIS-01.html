Release Version History Log

How to use this register
What & whyThis is the Release Version History Log register - the live, controlled record for this area. A blank register is a document; once populated it is a record.
Who maintains itThe owner of the related process keeps it current; entries are made as events occur.
How to completeOne row per item. Complete every column (the header row defines each column). Do not delete the header; add rows as needed; keep historical rows.
Update frequencyUpdate whenever an item is added or changes; review completeness at internal audit and management review.
What auditors checkCurrent, complete and consistent with the related SOP; entries traceable; under document control.
Pitfalls & controlDon't let it go stale or keep private copies. Control it per DOC-SOP-01; version by the date in the file/footer.
