HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT — guidance, delete before release
What this is & whyThis is the Prescription / Patient Specification Record, produced/used under CMD-SOP-01. It is the form/checklist that records this activity.
When & whoCreated when its parent procedure (CMD-SOP-01) requires it, by the responsible role in that process.
Before you start (inputs)Have ready: the relevant outputs of CMD-SOP-01, the applicable standards, and the product/version this applies to. Clone the template and replace [Organisation] and other [placeholders] throughout.
How to complete it - section by section• 1 Scope & Identification: State exactly which product/version/process this covers, and what it excludes.
• 2 Prescription: Complete with specific, evidence-based content; replace placeholders; mark non-applicable as 'n/a' with a reason.
Worked examplee.g. item 'Requirements testable' -> 'Y - every SRS item has an acceptance test (see SWD-TPL-05)'.
When it is done (outputs)Get it reviewed and approved per DOC-SOP-01; it then becomes a controlled record / technical-documentation item and feeds CMD-SOP-01. File it under document control.
What auditors checkCompleted and current; specific (not boilerplate); traceable to the product/version and to its evidence; consistent with the parent SOP; signed/dated where required.
Common mistakesticking items with no evidence; leaving items blank instead of 'n/a'; not signing/dating.
