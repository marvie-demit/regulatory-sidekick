1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure addresses risk management during IVD production. For software-only IVDs without physical production it is documented as a justified non-application; it applies where physical reagents/instruments are manufactured.

Scope: production-phase IVD risks (ISO 14971 §10), applied per RSK-SOP-01.
