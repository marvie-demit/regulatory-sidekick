1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures purchased products and services conform to requirements and that suppliers - including sub-processors of personal data and security-relevant services - are selected, evaluated and monitored.

Scope: all purchasing and outsourced processes. It satisfies ISO 13485 7.4, ISO 27001 A.5.19-23 and GDPR Art 28.

2 Roles & Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
QMROwns supplier approval and the supplier list.
Process Owner / BuyerDefines requirements, raises purchase orders and verifies incoming product/service.
Security / DPOAssesses security and data-protection of relevant suppliers (DPA/BAA).
