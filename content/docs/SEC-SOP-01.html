1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures product/security risks are managed across the lifecycle through a secure development lifecycle, vulnerability management and post-market security surveillance.

Scope: security of the device/software and the data it processes. It satisfies IEC 81001-5-1, MDCG 2019-16 and FDA 524B; security risk follows AAMI TIR57 as a branch of the risk file (RSK-SOP-01).
