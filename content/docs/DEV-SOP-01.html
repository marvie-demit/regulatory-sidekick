1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures problems found during development (incl. software anomalies) are recorded, investigated, resolved and trended.

Scope: development-phase problems and software anomalies. It satisfies IEC 62304 §9 and supports ISO 13485 7.3 and 8.5.
