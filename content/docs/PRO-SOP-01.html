When to useAcross all manufacturing/production activity. Dormant for software/IVD-only product.

How to use itPlan production with controlled conditions and approved work instructions, run the defined steps with in-process checks, keep device/lot traceability and production records, and control any nonconformities.
What auditors checkControlled conditions; approved work instructions; production & traceability records; special processes validated (PRO-SOP-03).
Pitfalls & relatedPitfalls: undocumented process changes; missing lot traceability. Related: PRO-SOP-03, PRO-TPL-01, NCP-SOP-01.

1 Purpose & Scope

Guidance (delete before release): 2-3 sentences: what this ensures and where it applies.

This procedure defines how [Organisation] manufactures product under controlled conditions so that every unit is made to the released specification and remains identifiable and traceable. It applies to all production and manufacturing activity, from work-order release through in-process control to batch release, and governs the controls placed on any outsourced manufacturer.
