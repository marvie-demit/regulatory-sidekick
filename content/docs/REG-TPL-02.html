HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT — guidance, delete before release
What this is & whyThis is the EU Authorised Representative Mandate, produced/used under REG-SOP-01. Completed and approved, it becomes a controlled record / technical-documentation item.
When & whoCreated when its parent procedure (REG-SOP-01) requires it, by the responsible role in that process.
Before you start (inputs)Have ready: the relevant outputs of REG-SOP-01, the applicable standards, and the product/version this applies to. Clone the template and replace [Organisation] and other [placeholders] throughout.
How to complete it - section by section• 1 Scope & Identification: State exactly which product/version/process this covers, and what it excludes.
• 2 Parties: Complete with specific, evidence-based content; replace placeholders; mark non-applicable as 'n/a' with a reason.
• 3 Scope (devices covered): State exactly which product/version/process this covers, and what it excludes.
• 4 Tasks of the Authorised Representative (Art 11(3)): Complete with specific, evidence-based content; replace placeholders; mark non-applicable as 'n/a' with a reason.
• 5 Liability for defective devices (Art 11(5)): Complete with specific, evidence-based content; replace placeholders; mark non-applicable as 'n/a' with a reason.
• 6 Signatures: Complete with specific, evidence-based content; replace placeholders; mark non-applicable as 'n/a' with a reason.
Worked examplee.g. don't write 'Scope: the scope of the device.' Write 'Scope: PlatoX v2.1 web app for clinicians; excludes the mobile viewer.'
When it is done (outputs)Get it reviewed and approved per DOC-SOP-01; it then becomes a controlled record / technical-documentation item and feeds REG-SOP-01. File it under document control.
What auditors checkCompleted and current; specific (not boilerplate); traceable to the product/version and to its evidence; consistent with the parent SOP; signed/dated where required.
Common mistakesrestating the heading instead of giving content; leaving [placeholders]; conclusions not traceable to evidence; sections left blank.
