1 Purpose

This policy states [Organisation]'s commitment to quality, safety, security, privacy and trustworthy AI, and provides the framework for setting quality objectives.

2 Our commitments

- Patient and user safety is our first priority in every decision.

- We meet all applicable regulatory and customer requirements (ISO 13485, MDR/IVDR, ISO 27001, ISO 42001, GDPR).

- We run one integrated, risk-based management system - quality, security, privacy and AI are managed together, not separately.

- We protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of data and systems.

- We develop and operate AI responsibly, with human oversight and ongoing performance monitoring.

- We continually improve the effectiveness of our QMS using data and feedback.

- We ensure our people are competent and aware of their contribution to quality and safety.

3 Review

This policy is communicated to all personnel and reviewed for continuing suitability at management review (MRV-SOP-01).
