1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure controls the design and development of products so outputs meet inputs, the device meets user needs and intended use, and a complete design file with traceability is maintained.

Scope: all product design & development (incl. software). It satisfies ISO 13485 7.3 and EU MDR Annex II. For software it runs jointly with SWD-SOP-01 and RSK-SOP-01.

3.1 Design & development planning

InputProduct concept; intended use
Activity• Prepare the D&D plan defining phases, deliverables, responsibilities and review gates.
• Define the verification, validation and design-transfer strategy and acceptance criteria.
• Plan interfaces between groups and required resources.
ResponsibleProject Manager (R)
Method / CriteriaISO 13485 7.3.2; risk-based phasing
Tools / TemplateDEV-TPL-07 D&D Plan
Output / RecordApproved D&D plan
ClauseISO 13485 7.3.2

3.2 Design inputs

InputIntended use; stakeholder & regulatory requirements
Activity• Capture design inputs: intended use, stakeholder requirements, regulatory/GSPR requirements and risk-derived requirements.
• Review the inputs for completeness, unambiguity, verifiability and absence of conflict.
• Approve the inputs before design proceeds.
ResponsibleDevelopment team (R)
Method / CriteriaInputs reviewed & approved (ISO 13485 7.3.3)
Tools / TemplateDEV-TPL-01 / DEV-TPL-02 / DEV-TPL-08
Output / RecordApproved design inputs
ClauseISO 13485 7.3.3

3.3 Design outputs

InputApproved design inputs
Activity• Produce design outputs (specifications, drawings, software) that meet the inputs.
• Provide information needed for purchasing, production/deployment and servicing.
• Define acceptance criteria; approve outputs before release.
ResponsibleDevelopment team (R)
Method / CriteriaOutputs meet inputs; approved before release
Tools / TemplateDEV-TPL-09 Design Specification
Output / RecordDesign outputs
ClauseISO 13485 7.3.4

3.4 Design reviews

InputPhase deliverables
Activity• Hold systematic design reviews at planned phase gates.
• Evaluate the ability of results to meet requirements and identify problems.
• Include reviewers independent of the stage being reviewed; record actions.
ResponsibleProject Manager (R)
Method / CriteriaReviews at planned stages
Tools / TemplateDEV-FOR-06 Design Review Checklist
Output / RecordDesign review records
ClauseISO 13485 7.3.5

3.5 Design verification

InputDesign outputs
Activity• Plan and perform verification that outputs meet inputs.
• Record objective evidence and resolve any failures.
ResponsibleDevelopment team (R)

Tools / TemplateSWD-TPL-11/12
Output / RecordVerification records

3.6 Design validation

InputVerified design
Activity• Validate that the device meets user needs and intended use under actual or representative conditions.
• Include usability validation (HUF) and clinical/performance evidence (CLE/PEV) as applicable.

ResponsibleDevelopment team (R); Clinical/Usability (Support)
Method / CriteriaValidation under actual/representative use

InputChange need
