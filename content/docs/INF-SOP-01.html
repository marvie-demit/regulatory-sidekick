1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures computerised systems and software tools used in the QMS, production or service are validated for their intended use, proportionate to risk.

Scope: software used in the QMS and tools affecting product quality (not the product itself). It satisfies ISO 13485 4.1.6 and follows ISO/TR 80002-2 and GAMP 5.
