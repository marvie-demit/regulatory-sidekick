1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures complaints are received, recorded, evaluated, investigated and resolved, and that reportable events and corrective actions are triggered.

Scope: all complaints about marketed product or service. It satisfies ISO 13485 8.2.2 and feeds vigilance (MDR Art 87).

2 Roles & Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
QMROwns complaint handling and the complaint log; ensures timely evaluation.
Process OwnerInvestigates and resolves complaints in their area.
RA / PMS OwnerDetermines reportability and feeds PMS.

3 Procedure

Guidance (delete before release): Each step is a structured configuration (IDEF0/ICOM): Input, Activity, Responsible (RACI), Method/Criteria (Control), Tools/Template (Mechanism), Output/Record, Clause.

3.1 Receive & record

InputComplaint from any channel
Activity• Record the complaint with all relevant detail in the complaint log.
• Acknowledge receipt to the complainant where appropriate.
• Capture all complaints without screening any out.
ResponsibleQMR (R)
Method / CriteriaAll complaints captured
Tools / TemplateCMP-LIS-01 Complaint Log
Output / RecordComplaint record
ClauseISO 13485 8.2.2

3.2 Evaluate & triage

InputComplaint record
Activity• Assess the seriousness and potential harm.
• Determine whether the event may be reportable and set the priority.
• Decide whether investigation is required (record justification if not).
ResponsibleQMR (R); RA (Support)
Method / CriteriaReportability criteria (MDR Art 87)
Tools / TemplateCMP-LIS-01
Output / RecordEvaluation
ClauseISO 13485 8.2.2

3.3 Investigate

InputTriaged complaint
Activity• Investigate to root cause (link CAPA where systemic).
• Determine whether a nonconformity, risk update or field action is needed.
ResponsibleProcess Owner (R)
Method / CriteriaStructured RCA
Tools / TemplateCAP-SOP-01
Output / RecordInvestigation
ClauseISO 13485 8.2.2

3.4 Determine reportability

InputInvestigation
Activity• Decide whether the event is a reportable serious incident / FSCA.
• Route reportable events to vigilance within the applicable timelines.
ResponsibleRA (R)
Method / CriteriaMDR Art 87 criteria & timelines
Tools / TemplateVIG-SOP-02
Output / RecordReportability decision
ClauseMDR Art 87

3.5 Respond & close

InputResolution
Activity• Respond to the complainant.
• Raise CAPA where needed and feed the complaint into PMS.
• Close the complaint when all actions are complete.
ResponsibleQMR (R)
Method / CriteriaTimely response; closure on completion
Tools / TemplateCMP-LIS-01; CAP-SOP-01
Output / RecordClosed complaint
ClauseISO 13485 8.2.2
