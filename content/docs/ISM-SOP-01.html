1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure establishes and maintains an ISMS protecting the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information and systems.

Scope: the organisation's information assets and IT/cloud environment. It satisfies ISO/IEC 27001 and SOC 2, using controls from ISO/IEC 27002/27017/27018 and ISO 27799 (health), with risk per ISO/IEC 27005.

2 Roles & Responsibilities
