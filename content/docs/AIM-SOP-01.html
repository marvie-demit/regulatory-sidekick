1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure governs the lifecycle of AI/ML used in the device so it is safe, effective, transparent and subject to human oversight and ongoing monitoring.

Scope: all AI/ML components. As a medical-device AI it is HIGH-RISK under the EU AI Act, so the full Annex IV technical documentation applies. It satisfies EU AI Act and ISO/IEC 42001, follows FDA GMLP, and integrates AI risk into the risk file (RSK-SOP-01, AI Act Art 9).
