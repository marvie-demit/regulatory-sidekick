1 Purpose & Scope

This procedure ensures the infrastructure needed to achieve product conformity and service availability - facilities, IT and cloud environments, and equipment - is provided, maintained and monitored.

Scope: all infrastructure and computerised systems supporting product and QMS, including cloud hosting. It satisfies ISO 13485 6.3 and supports ISO 27001 Annex A (asset & operations) and service availability.

2 Roles & Responsibilities

RoleResponsibility
ManagementDetermines and provides the required infrastructure.
IT / Infrastructure OwnerMaintains and monitors infrastructure incl. cloud; keeps the inventory current.
QMREnsures maintenance is planned and recorded.
